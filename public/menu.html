Our Special Tacos

Discover the best one for you!

Al Pastor Taco

Originating from the influence of Lebanese immigrants in Mexico, Al Pastor tacos feature marinated pork cooked on a vertical spit, reminiscent of Middle Eastern shawarma. The meat is seasoned with a blend of spices, including dried chilies, achiote, and pineapple, for a touch of sweetness. The result is tender, flavorful pork with a slightly smoky and tangy taste. Al Pastor tacos are incredibly popular for their unique savory and sweet flavor.

Arabes Taco

Similar to Al Pastor, Arabes tacos also draw inspiration from Arabian cuisine. These tacos feature thinly sliced, marinated pork that is also cooked on a vertical spit. However, the main difference lies in the marinade, which often includes different spices and herbs, giving Arabes tacos a distinct flavor profile. The specific taste can vary depending on the recipe and regional variations, but overall, Arabes tacos offer a delightful fusion of Middle Eastern and Mexican flavors.

Barbacoa Taco

Barbacoa tacos boast a rich history, with origins tracing back to traditional Mexican cooking techniques. Typically made from slow-cooked beef or lamb, the meat is cooked underground, imparting a unique smoky taste and tender texture. The resulting meat is incredibly flavorful, with a melt-in-your-mouth quality. Barbacoa tacos are trendy for their savory, earthy flavor and the delectable combination of tender meat and aromatic spices.

Birria Taco

Birria tacos are a beloved Mexican delicacy. Hailing from the state of Jalisco, it is made with goat or beef meat marinated in a blend of spices. It is then slow-cooked until tender and served in warm tortillas. Birria tacos are famous for their rich and robust flavor, complemented by a consommé dipping sauce, which adds an extra depth of savory goodness.

Carne Asada Taco

Carne Asada tacos showcase the essence of grilled meat perfection. This classic style features thinly sliced, marinated steak, typically prepared with a combination of citrus juices, garlic, and various spices. The meat is grilled, creating a tantalizing smoky flavor while maintaining its juicy tenderness. Carne Asada tacos are adored for their bold and robust taste, heightened by the charred edges and the combination of tangy marinade and char-grilled goodness.

Carnitas Taco

Carnitas tacos originate from Michoacán, Mexico, and showcase the marvel of slow-cooked, succulent pork. To prepare the dish, pork is simmered in its own fat until it is tender and caramelized. The result is a symphony of flavors and textures—a harmony between succulent, melt-in-your-mouth meat and an irresistible crunch. Carnitas tacos are celebrated for their rich and savory flavor profile.

Sides

Grilled Corn on the Cob

Grilled corn on the cob, or "elote," is a quintessential Mexican street food that bursts with flavor. Charred to perfection over an open flame, the sweet corn kernels are slathered with creamy mayonnaise, sprinkled with crumbled cotija cheese, and dusted with chili powder. A squeeze of fresh lime adds a zesty finish, creating a deliciously smoky, tangy, and savory treat that's perfect for any occasion. Enjoy this popular delicacy at barbecues, festivals, or as a mouthwatering side dish to your favorite Mexican meals.

Guacamole and Tortilla Chips

Guacamole, a beloved Mexican classic, is a creamy and flavorful dip made from ripe avocados, tangy lime juice, fresh cilantro, diced tomatoes, and a hint of jalapeño. Paired with crispy tortilla chips, it's a harmonious blend of creamy, zesty, and spicy flavors that elevate any gathering. Whether served as an appetizer, snack, or topping for tacos and burritos, guacamole and tortilla chips are a crowd-pleasing favorite that's perfect for sharing with friends and family.

Churros with Chocolate Sauce

Churros with chocolate sauce are a delectable dessert that originates from Spain and has become a beloved treat in Mexican cuisine. These golden-brown, crispy pastries are coated in a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar, creating a delightful contrast of textures. Dip them in the rich, velvety chocolate sauce for an indulgent combination of sweetness and warmth. Perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings, churros with chocolate sauce are a delightful way to end any meal on a high note.

Drinks

Stay refreshed with our selection of drinks!

Horchata

Horchata is a traditional Mexican beverage with a creamy and refreshing flavor profile. Made from rice, milk, and cinnamon, this delicious drink is sweetened with a touch of vanilla and sugar, resulting in a smooth and satisfying taste. Served chilled, horchata is the perfect thirst-quencher on a hot day, providing a delightful balance of flavors and a hint of nostalgia with every sip.

Aguas Frescas

Aguas Frescas are a refreshing and hydrating category of beverages commonly enjoyed in Mexican cuisine. Made from a blend of fresh fruits, water, and a hint of sweetness, these drinks offer a burst of natural flavors. From classic favorites like watermelon and hibiscus to innovative combinations, aguas frescas provide a delightful and revitalizing experience, perfect for staying cool and refreshed on warm days.

Jamaica Agua Fresca

Jamaica Agua Fresca is a vibrant and refreshing beverage made from dried hibiscus flowers. With its ruby-red hue and tangy, floral flavor, it's a popular choice for quenching thirst in the heat. Served chilled, Jamaica Agua Fresca offers a delightful balance of tartness and sweetness, making it a refreshing and healthful alternative to sugary drinks. Its natural antioxidants and distinctive taste make it a beloved choice for those seeking a unique and revitalizing beverage.
