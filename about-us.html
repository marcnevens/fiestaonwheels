About us

Welcome to Fiesta on Wheels, your go-to destination for the most mouth-watering tacos on wheels! We are more than just a taco truck; we are a family dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors of Mexico to your neighborhood. Our team is a diverse blend of culinary enthusiasts, each bringing their unique touch to create an unforgettable taco experience.

Together, we are just like a Fiesta on Wheels - a team united by a love for tacos and a commitment to bringing joy to our community. Join us on our culinary adventure and let us spice up your day with the best tacos in town!

Meet our chef - Alejandro Rodríguez

Our chef Alejandro Rodríguez is the culinary genius behind our food truck's irresistible Mexican cuisine. Born and raised in the heart of Mexico City, Chef Alejandro's passion for cooking was ignited at a young age in his grandmother's bustling kitchen. It was there he learned the secrets of authentic Mexican flavors, mastering traditional recipes passed down through generations.

After honing his skills in some of Mexico City's finest restaurants, Chef Alejandro decided to bring his culinary expertise to the streets, infusing every dish with the vibrant, bold tastes of his heritage. His culinary journey led him to our food truck, where he transforms fresh, locally-sourced ingredients into mouth-watering masterpieces.

From sizzling tacos al pastor to savory burritos and crispy churros, every bite from Chef Alejandro's kitchen is a testament to his dedication and love for Mexican cuisine. His innovative approach marries tradition with modern flair, creating a menu that not only delights the palate but also tells a story of rich cultural heritage and culinary excellence.
